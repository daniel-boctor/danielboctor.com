FAQ

I am seeing conflicts between this data and data on other sites (portfoliovisualizer, etc). How come?

It is not uncommon to see discrepancies in data between different sites. Our methodology is as follows. All data used is inclusive of all dividends and distributions payed out during the sample. In addition to this, if daily granularity is selected, the data will span the entire sample. This is from the first available close, right up until the most recent trading day, and will use the current intra-day price during market hours. It is also imporant to note that if multiple securities have different inception dates, the most recent one will be used for the entire sample.

What's next?

Style analysis, dynamic weighting schemes, stochastic models, portfolio insurance, and machine learning models are all in the works.

Why are some tasks so slow?

At the moment, the entire website is being hosted on a raspberry pi. Because of this, a lot of tasks, especially neural networks, are very slow. I will be upgrading to a faster server sometime in a near future.
